Autonomous Person Following Robot

Winter 2022

Our iRobot in action.

For my MECH 464 industrial robotics course, I used an iRobot and an Intel RGBD camera to build a person following cobot. I used OpenCV to track the person, and PID control to follow them. For technical details of the project, see the report below. All of our code is accessible here.
